# 🖼️ bigblue terminal font

bigblue terminal font takes me back to the good old days of computing. it's retro, it's fixed-width, and it's just plain cool.
i use it in my menu bar because i find it works best for decorative purposes. it's not the most readable font, but it's great for adding a touch of style to your desktop.
